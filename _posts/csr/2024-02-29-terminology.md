### ALPIDE
ALPIDE is short form of ALice PIxel Detector. 
- Dimension: $1.5\mathrm{cm} \times 3.0\mathrm{cm}$
- Pixels: $512(row) \times 1024(column)$
- Pixel pitches
  - Old: $28\mathrm{\mu m} \times 28 \mathrm{\mu m}$
